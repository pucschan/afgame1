Understanding Intangible Assets

An intangible asset is an asset that is not physical in nature. You cannot touch it, see it, or hold it, but it holds significant value for a business (often helping them generate revenue long-term).

Tangible Assets (Physical)

  • Buildings & Factories
  • Company Vehicles
  • Computers & Office Furniture
  • Cash & Inventory

Intangible Assets (Non-Physical)

  • Brand Reputation / Goodwill
  • Copyrights & Patents
  • Trademarks (Logos/Names)
  • Proprietary Software & Code
